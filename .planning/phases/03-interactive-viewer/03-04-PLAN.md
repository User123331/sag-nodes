<objective>
Fix the three compounding engine bugs that cause MusicBrainz 503 rate-limit floods and
Deezer numeric IDs being stored as MBIDs — corrupting subsequent expand calls with 400s.

Purpose: Heavy double-click expansion currently floods MusicBrainz with parallel URL-lookup
requests bypassing RequestQueue, and falls back to raw Deezer IDs when resolution fails —
making expand() send garbage IDs to every downstream API.

Output: resolveUrlToMbid is queue-throttled; unresolvable artists are silently filtered;
expand() rejects non-UUID arguments immediately.
</objective>
